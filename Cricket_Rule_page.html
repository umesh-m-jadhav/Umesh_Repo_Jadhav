2. General Rules

1. The league will be organized in an IPL-style format. All registered players will be from Trendy Tower, while up to 20% of the players will be invited from other towers. These invited players will be considered as ‘foreign players’.
 
2. League will be played in round robin way.
 
3. Top 4 team will be consider for semi final.
 
4. We are planning to appoint an external professional umpire for this league. In case a professional umpire is unavailable, an internal member will perform the role of umpire

3. Bowler's rule

1. Throw bowling is not permitted. On the first instance, it will be declared a No Ball and treated as a warning. A second instance will also be declared a No Ball, after which the bowler will be banned from bowling further in that match.
 
2. Maximum 2 over per bowler will be allowed.

4. Dress Code

1. Dress code is mandatory. Only full sports pants and T-shirts are permitted during play. Shorts, formal wear, or any other attire will not be allowed. Players not adhering to the dress code will not be permitted to participate in the match.

5. Declared zone & Power over

1. Certain areas of the ground will be designated as declared zone to count as 1 or 2 runs. A zone board will be displayed on the ground to indicate these areas.
2. One over will be designated as the Power Over, during which all runs scored will be counted as double.
By participating in this tournament all players and officials agree to abide by these rules and any subsequent playing conditions issued by the Tournament Committee.
